Sorry, we cannot log you in to the service

If you’re using Edge, try changing to a different browser such as Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Apple Safari or Opera. Then launch the SR1 service again.

If you still cannot log in, contact your IT department.

Alternatively, you can complete a PDF version of the SR1 form and the fee claim form which you can email back to us.

The PDF versions do not need to be signed, but you’ll need to return them to us from a secure email address. Any email that does not come from a secure email address will be automatically deleted.

Secure email addresses end in:

  • @nhs.net
  • @nhs.uk
  • @nhs.scot
  • @nhs.wales.uk
  • @gov.uk

Send the request for a PDF version of the SR1 form, and fee claim form if needed, from your secure email address to form.e-sr1@dwp.gov.uk.

Please include your:

  • surgery/hospital/postal address
  • phone number

Do not encrypt any part of the email.
