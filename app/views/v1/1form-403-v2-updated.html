The IT admin or team where you work should be able to support you, so contact them if possible.

If you cannot contact them right now, there are some things you can try.

Clear your cookies

Sometimes cookies can stop you from accessing a website. Clearing them can often resolve the problem.

How to clear cookies in Edge

  1. Stay on this page and move your cursor to the address bar at the top of the screen. The address bar will contain a web address starting with https://ds1500.
  2. Click the padlock icon on the left of the web address.
  3. From the drop-down menu, select Cookies and site data.
  4. You’ll see a list of the cookies in use. Select the first cookie and press Remove.
  5. If there is more than one cookie, press Remove until all the cookies are cleared.
  6. Press Done.
  7. Click Send an SR1 at the top of this page. This will take you back to the page where you can launch the service again.

You should be able to log on to the SR1 service now, but you might have to repeat these steps next time.

If you still cannot access the service, try using a different browser, and check that your role is authorised to use this service.

Use a different browser

If you’re using Edge, try accessing the SR1 service using Chrome. To open Chrome, go to your device's start menu and look for the Chrome logo.

Check your role is authorised

Only these job roles are authorised to complete an SR1 form:

  • GP
  • hospital consultant
  • specialty doctor
  • hospice doctor
  • senior specialist nurse (such as a clinical nurse specialist, advanced nurse practitioner or similar)

Your job role is checked against the job role on your NHS smartcard. If you should have access to this service, make sure your smartcard is correct. If it’s wrong, your IT support team will be able to help.

If you do not have an account to log incidents with the NHS National Service Desk call 0300 303 5035.

Secure email addresses end in:

Send the request for a PDF version of the SR1 form, and fee claim form if needed, from your secure email address to form.e-sr1@dwp.gov.uk.

  • surgery /hospital /postal address
